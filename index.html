Eleanor Brinegar

Legal Secretary

I am a prospective law student currently getting my Bachelor's Degree in Philosophy from University of California, Riverside. I am currently working at the law firm of Lester, Cantrell & Kraus, LLP as a Legal Secretary for Gary A. Foltz, who I have been working with for over two years. Although I am just nearing the end of my first year at University, I have years of experience in the workforce and a longstanding passion for law. I captained my High School Mock Trial Team for three years over which I acquired many county awards for my performance as a trial attorney, pre trial attorney, and witness.

My experience as a legal secretary has taught me how a law firm is operated and has helped me gain the skills necessary to efficiently work with coworkers, draft documents, and communicate with clients, among other things. The attorney I work for practices estate, corporaate, and tax law. I personally manage his nearly 200 corporate accounts, keeping them updated on their minutes and drafting important agreements and record keeping documents.

My educational journey with law began my freshman year of high school when I was encouraged by a teacher to join the school's mock trial team. I was the first freshman to ever be trained into a pretrial attorney position and although the position was quite difficult, I received recognition for my performance in the role. I soon after became captain of the team and continued to hone my skills in criminal law as well as train incoming students and lead the team through each season of competition. Because of this experience and my love of philosophy, I decided to pursue my degree in philosophy emphasis law and society with the intention of attending law school and eventually becoming an attorney of civil law.
